About Us

In 2007, the highest grossing film was Spider-Man 3, Tim Reddy had an @aol.com email address, and "Sound Fix," a bar in Brooklyn, held music trivia every Tuesday night.

Stephen and his group of friends attended religiously and a major reason for their attendance was Mona, who wrote the questions, worked the bar - and was generous with buy-backs.


"I ♥ Nilsson" graffiti from the Sound Fix bathroom

One night Stephen got to the bar early. He saw Mona's book behind the counter - she was reading Revolutionary Road. Stephen was reading Hemmingway's Garden of Eden - a book where a wife strong-arms her husband into cutting and bleaching his hair to match hers and then violates him.
Ah yes - romance perfumed the night air.


This woman will emasculate this man - out of love.

courting is in session

Weeks later, at Music Trivia - Stephen invited Mona to a party (that he never ended up throwing) - to celebrate the 25 year anniversary of The Kinks album "Village Green Preservation Society." Mona sounded enthusiastic enough and passed a note to Stephen with her email address and this caveat: "I like Dave Davies more than Ray Davies."


Dave & Ray Davies, who have never been emasculated by their wives.

we've always been more than just good friends

Stephen and Mona became better friends. Throughout the year they:

  • Made venn diagrams
  • Assembled IKEA furniture
  • Defaced IKEA manuals
  • Tried to learn this dance
  • Watched a lot of Battlestar Galactica
  • Listened to these these two songs and thought of each other.

Brevity is...wit

We found out that we liked each other so much that we're getting married.
